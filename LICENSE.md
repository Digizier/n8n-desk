## Our promise

alike. No asterisks, no seat counts, no per-user fees, no "talk to sales" dialogs in
the way of the things that matter.

This isn't a marketing claim. It's the license.

## Why this license exists

We took inspiration from n8n's own [Sustainable Use License](https://docs.n8n.io/sustainable-use-license/),
because the philosophy fits n8n-desk perfectly:

> Open enough to thrive as a community project. Sustainable enough to keep being
> built, maintained, and supported for the long run.

Pure permissive licenses (MIT, Apache) make it trivial for someone to fork the
project, repaint it, and ship it as their own paid app. Pure proprietary licenses
kill the community before it starts. The Sustainable Use approach threads the needle:
**the software is free; only the act of wrapping n8n-desk and selling it as your own
product is reserved.**

That's what we're doing here. n8n-desk belongs to its users and contributors. The
people maintaining it just want to make sure it can keep being maintained.

## The one thing we ask

**Don't wrap n8n-desk and sell it as your own product.**

n8n-desk is a desktop and mobile app, and the concern for an app is straightforward:
forking it, rebranding it, and shipping it as your own paid app — whether on an app
store, as a download, or bundled into a commercial product where n8n-desk is the
substance behind a different name. If that's what you want to do, please reach out
first. We'll either work something out or wave you through; what we want to avoid is
the project being commoditized away from the community that built it.

To make the difference concrete:

- **Fine, no permission needed:** A consultancy that installs, configures, or
  customizes n8n-desk for clients and charges for that work.
- **Fine, no permission needed:** Selling plugins, themes, workflow templates,
  agent prompts, or integrations that work with n8n-desk.
- **Fine, no permission needed:** Using n8n-desk inside your own company, or
  recommending it inside a paid product, course, or service you're already running.
- **Fine, no permission needed:** Forking n8n-desk to fix bugs, add features,
  or run an internal version — even if your company is a commercial business.
- **Please reach out first:** Forking n8n-desk, putting your own branding on it,
  and shipping it as your own commercial app or product. That includes paid app
  store releases, paid downloads, and bundling n8n-desk as the headline component
  of a paid product under a different name.

The line is "the value you sell is **on top of** n8n-desk" (fine) versus "the value
you sell **is** n8n-desk, repainted" (please ask). If you're not sure which side
your project sits on, write to us at [n8n-desk.app](https://n8n-desk.app). We'd
rather have a five-minute conversation than a misunderstanding.
